Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services as soon as possible after your engagement, especially if you're planning a wedding during peak season (May-October). For full-service planning, 9-12 months in advance is ideal. For month-of coordination, we suggest booking at least 6 months before your wedding date to ensure availability.

Do you work with a specific vendor list?

While we have established relationships with many trusted vendors in the DFW area, we don't limit our clients to a specific vendor list. We provide recommendations based on your style, budget, and preferences, but you're always free to choose your own vendors. If you bring in vendors we haven't worked with before, we'll coordinate with them to ensure everything runs smoothly.

How many weddings do you take on each month?

To ensure each couple receives the attention and service they deserve, we limit ourselves to a maximum of four weddings per month. This allows us to provide personalized service and dedicated attention to each of our clients.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We work with a wide range of budgets and can customize our services to meet your specific needs. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget constraints and help you prioritize what's most important to you, ensuring you get the most value for your investment.

What happens if you're unavailable on my wedding date?

If we're already booked for your wedding date, we'll be happy to recommend other reputable wedding planners in the area who may be available. We maintain relationships with several trusted colleagues and can help you find someone who matches your style and needs.
