Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit my contact form?

After submitting your contact form, you can expect to hear back from us within 24-48 hours. We'll reach out to schedule an initial consultation, which can be conducted in person, over the phone, or via video call, depending on your preference.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, we offer complimentary initial consultations. This gives us a chance to get to know each other, discuss your vision, and determine if we're a good fit for your wedding planning needs.

Do you travel for weddings outside the DFW area?

Yes, we do! While we're based in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, we're happy to travel for destination weddings or events in other locations. Additional travel fees may apply depending on the location.

How soon should I contact you before my wedding date?

We recommend reaching out as soon as possible after your engagement, especially if you're interested in our full-service planning package. For month-of coordination, contacting us 6-8 months before your wedding date is ideal to ensure availability.

What if I need to reschedule my consultation?

We understand that schedules can change. If you need to reschedule your consultation, simply contact us at least 24 hours in advance, and we'll be happy to find a new time that works for you.
